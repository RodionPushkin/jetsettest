LEGAL COMPLIANCE

All transactions are done in full compliance with existing legal requirements on assets’ ownership transfer.

  • KYC/KYB/AML

    Only checked users can participate in transaction

  • Digital signatures

    All documents are signed with legally-binding eSignatures in accordance with eIDAS standards

  • Legal agreements

    Purchase agreements, closing and other documents are pre-drafted by top-tier lawyers to provide consistency and safety of transactions

  • Immutable storage

    All digital documents on ownership and title transfer are immutably stored onchain on Interplanetary File System (IPFS) providing total safety

SMART CONTRACTS

The flow of transactions, covenants and funds transfer are secured by smart contracts on blockchain. No need for escrow agents or other middlemen to control the transaction.
